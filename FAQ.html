    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Sarv Kalyanam Samiti is a non-profit organization dedicated to the social, moral, character, physical, cultural, and mental development of individuals to create competent citizens for the nation.

    The office of Sarv Kalyanam Samiti is located at Stuti Villa, S-26, K.K. Puri Colony, Nandanpura, Jhansi.

    The objectives include promoting overall development of individuals, establishing educational institutions without discrimination, setting up higher educational institutions, developing research laboratories, and providing medical and paramedical services among others.

    You can join Sarv Kalyanam Samiti by filling out the membership form available on our website under the 'Join Us' tab. Membership is open to all individuals interested in our objectives and mission.

    Members benefit from participating in various developmental programs, receiving regular updates about our activities, and having the opportunity to contribute to societal betterment through our initiatives.

    The society establishes educational institutions at various levels, including schools and higher educational institutions, without any discrimination of caste, religion, or language.

    The society develops and equips laboratories with modern scientific resources for technical research, and sets up facilities for government and non-government research.

    Yes, Sarv Kalyanam Samiti participates in various government and NITI Aayog schemes according to regulations and implements training programs under these schemes.

    The society provides medical, nursing, and other paramedical services for societal development and emergency situations.

    You can volunteer with Sarv Kalyanam Samiti by reaching out to us through the contact information provided on our website. We welcome volunteers who are passionate about contributing to our mission and objectives.

    SARV KALYANAM SAMITI

    Sarv Kalyanam Samiti, based in Jhansi, is a dedicated organization committed to the welfare and development of society. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals.
